BLACKPINK

Blackpink is a South Korean girl group founded by YG Entertainment. Members include Jisoo, Jennie, Rose, and Lisa. The Korean girl group known as the "biggest girl group in the world" is a global success and a driving force in the Korean Wave. They are stylistically associated with the "girl crush" notion in K-pop, embracing themes of female strength and self-confidence.


Blackpink made their debut in August 2016 with their single album Square One, featuring the number-one hits "Whistle" and "Boombayah" on the Gaon Digital Chart in South Korea and the Billboard World Digital Songs chart in the US. In November, the single album Square Two was released, and the song "Playing with Fire" became the first by a Korean girl group to reach the Billboard Canadian Hot 100. In 2018, the group's song "Ddu-Du Ddu-Du" became the first by a Korean female group to hit the UK Singles Chart and get certification in the US, expanding their global influence. The Korean group's music video was the first to reach one and two billion views on YouTube, and it still holds the record for the most views by a Korean group. The music videos for "Kill This Love" (2019) and "How You Like That" (2020) broke Guinness World Records for the highest number of views in the first 24 hours.

Blackpink is the highest-charting female Korean act on the US Billboard Hot 100, reaching number 13 with "Ice Cream" (2020) and number one with "Born Pink" (2022). Blackpink's studio albums, The Album (2020) and Born Pink, became the best-selling female albums in South Korea, selling over one million and two million copies, respectively.Born Pink was the first album by a girl group to reach number one on the Billboard 200 since Danity Kane in 2008. It also set two Guinness World Records for being the first album by a Korean girl group to top both the Billboard 200 and the UK Albums Chart. The album's debut single, "Pink Venom" (2022), became the first song by a Korean group to achieve number one on Australia's ARIA Singles Chart and the first by a girl group to hit number one on the Billboard Global 200.


Blackpink has set multiple milestones, including the highest-grossing concert tour by a female group (Born Pink World Tour, 2022-23) and the first Asian act to headline Coachella. They have the most subscribed and viewed music artist channel on YouTube, as well as the most followed and streamed girl group on Spotify. Blackpink has received numerous awards, including several Golden Disc Awards, Billboard Music Awards, People's Choice Awards, and MTV Video Music Awards. They were also the first girl group to win Group of the Year in the twenty-first century. They were named Time's 2022 Entertainer of the Year and the first girl group to make Forbes' 30 Under 30 Asia list. The trio has been recognized by former South Korean President Moon Jae-in for spreading K-pop and Korean culture globally, ranking among the top Forbes Korea Power Celebrities.
